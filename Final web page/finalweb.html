"This page is for all of the MK fans who have enjoyed the movies and games for what they are. So that they can immerse themselves into Mortal Kombat and enjoy things such as their favorite characters." -Page Developer

Mortal Kombat is a series of player vs. player fighting games. Its uniqness stems from its utilization of graphic violence amongst other typical arcade games. Mortal Kombat debuted to arcades in early 90's. Since then Mortal Kombat has been made widespread in multiple adaptations, like comics, movies, and platform games. Its most recent adaptation, Mortal Kombat 1, released earlier this year in September. The fans were in excitement to the recent game which has introduced several 3D era characters and famous ones suchs as Omni-Man from Amazon Primes Original Invincible.

The video game current timeline includes Mortal Kombat X, Mortal Kombat 11 and Mortal Kombat 1. This collective timeline emphasizes a mutiversal battle between the realms with Liu Kang and Raiden playing important roles in these stories. In the recent film adaptation that relased in theaters 2021 depicted the relationship between Scorpion and Sub-Zero with the introduction of a new main character Cole Young

Story

Some of the characters above have been reintroduced in the Mortal Kombat 1 universe. For some its been years since their last appearance on the roster of fighters. Fire God Liu Kang has created a new timeline seperate from the Mortal Kombat 11 timeline. The new tournament to discover the realms protectors has begun and only one champion will prevail. Liu Kang has recruited two earthrealm(earth) fighters, Kung Lao and Raiden. The Earthrealm team travel to Edenia, an empire located in the realm of outworld. As the reigning empress of Edenia, Sindel is tasked with creating a team as well. The Edenian empire has fought in the tournament for hundreds of years and have prepared their fighters thoroughly. Edenias fighters include Sindels daughter Mileena, as well as General Shao. The games have and undertone of deceit and mistrust as it comes to light that Shang Tsung has infiltrated the tournament in attempts of conquering the realms.
