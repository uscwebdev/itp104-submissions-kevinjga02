Mortal Kombat is a series of player vs. player fighting games. Its uniqness stems from its utilization of graphic violence amongst other typical arcade games. Mortal Kombat debuted to arcades in early 90's. Since then Mortal Kombat has been made widespread in multiple adaptations, like comics, movies, and platform games. Its most recent adaptation, Mortal Kombat 1, released earlier this year in September. The fans were in excitement to the recent game which has introduced several 3D era characters and famous ones suchs as Omni-Man from Amazon Primes Original Invincible.

The video game current timeline includes Mortal Kombat X, Mortal Kombat 11 and Mortal Kombat 1. This collective timeline emphasizes a mutiversal battle between the realms with Liu Kang and Raiden playing important roles in these stories. In the recent film adaptation that relased in theaters 2021 depicted the relationship between Scorpion and Sub-Zero with the introduction of a new main character Cole Young
